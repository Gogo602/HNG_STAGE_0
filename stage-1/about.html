Extra Thoughts and Reflections

The HNG program presents a unique blend of high pressure and rapid learning, perfectly simulating real-world agile development environments. My observation is that success hinges not just on technical aptitude, but on soft skills—specifically effective communication, time management, and the ability to search and debug independently. The transition from building controlled, static components (Stage 0) to complex, validated, multi-page applications (Stage 1) reinforces the value of semantic structure and attention to accessibility details, principles I intend to carry into all future professional projects.
